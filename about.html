Philosophy

This is a documentation project developed inorder to aid students learn how compiler design concepts are implemented practically.

It focuses on 'in-depth' explanation of the concepts used. This, we beleive, is very important and can be very useful in other areas of software engineering as well. It has been initiated and developed by Dr. K. Muralikrishnan inorder to increase the availability of reference material for the compiler design laboratory and to reduce the number of unanswered doubts that rise in a developer's mind. This project aims at providing a strong foundation of basic knowledge in compiler development.

Authors

The content in the website and the documentation has been authored in the Computer Science and Engineering Department, National Institute of Technology, Calicut in the year 2013, under the guidance of Dr. K. Muralikrishnan by Nachiappan.V and Arun Rajan (2010-14 Batch)
